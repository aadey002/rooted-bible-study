Week 7: Eternity and Mission

Judgments, Heaven, Hell, Eternity, Our Mission

Overview

This final week examines eternal destinies and our mission as believers until Christ returns.

1. Judgments

God will judge all people. Believers will be judged for rewards at the Judgment Seat of Christ, while unbelievers face the Great White Throne.

"For we must all appear before the judgment seat of Christ, so that each one may receive what is due for what he has done in the body, whether good or evil." - 2 Corinthians 5:10

2. Heaven

Heaven is the eternal dwelling place of God where believers will live forever in His presence, free from sin, sorrow, and death.

"He will wipe away every tear from their eyes, and death shall be no more, neither shall there be mourning, nor crying, nor pain anymore." - Revelation 21:4

3. Hell

Hell is the eternal place of punishment for those who reject Christ. It is separation from God forever.

"And these will go away into eternal punishment, but the righteous into eternal life." - Matthew 25:46

4. Eternity

God will create a new heaven and new earth where righteousness dwells, and believers will reign with Christ forever.

"Then I saw a new heaven and a new earth, for the first heaven and the first earth had passed away." - Revelation 21:1

5. Our Mission

Until Christ returns, we are called to proclaim the gospel, make disciples, and live holy lives that glorify God.

"But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit has come upon you, and you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the end of the earth." - Acts 1:8
