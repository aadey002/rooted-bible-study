About This Curriculum

Wisdom is a comprehensive Bible study curriculum by Pastor Brian Bailey, covering 22 essential doctrines of the Christian faith.

Whether you're preparing for ministry, discipling new believers, or deepening your own understanding of the faith, this curriculum provides a solid foundation rooted in Scripture.
