About This Curriculum

Rooted is a comprehensive Bible study curriculum developed by Adetoro Oriaifo, based on the ordination preparation document of Pastor Brian Ellis Bailey.

The curriculum transforms dense theological reference material into an engaging, accessible educational experience while maintaining complete theological accuracy and biblical documentation. Every doctrine is supported by multiple Scripture references from the original source.

Whether you're preparing for ministry, discipling new believers, or deepening your own understanding of the faith, Rooted provides a solid foundation in essential Christian doctrine.
