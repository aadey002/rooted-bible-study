About the Course

The Church Doctrinal Statement of Faith Bible Course is a comprehensive 7-week curriculum covering 22 essential Christian doctrines. It is designed for individual study, small groups, Sunday school classes, and discipleship programs.

Pastor Brian Ellis Bailey

Pastor Bailey developed this curriculum to help believers grow in their understanding of foundational Christian doctrine.

For questions or speaking invitations, please reach out through your church leadership.
