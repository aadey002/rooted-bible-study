Week 2: Humanity and Salvation

Angels, Satan and Demons, Man, Sin, Salvation

Overview

This week explores the spiritual realm, human nature, the problem of sin, and Gods plan of salvation.

1. Angels

Angels are created spiritual beings who serve God and minister to believers. They are messengers and servants of the Most High.

"Are they not all ministering spirits sent out to serve for the sake of those who are to inherit salvation?" - Hebrews 1:14

2. Satan and Demons

Satan is a fallen angel who leads a rebellion against God. Demons are fallen angels who serve Satan but are defeated enemies.

"Be sober-minded; be watchful. Your adversary the devil prowls around like a roaring lion, seeking someone to devour." - 1 Peter 5:8

3. Man

Humanity was created in the image of God, with inherent dignity and worth. We were made for relationship with God.

"So God created man in his own image, in the image of God he created him; male and female he created them." - Genesis 1:27

4. Sin

Sin entered the world through Adam and has affected all humanity. All have sinned and fall short of Gods glory.

"For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." - Romans 3:23

5. Salvation

Salvation is by grace alone through faith alone in Christ alone. It is a gift of God, not earned by works.

"For by grace you have been saved through faith. And this is not your own doing; it is the gift of God." - Ephesians 2:8
