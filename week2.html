D

Man Created & Fallen

Made in God's Image, Lost Through Sin

Core Truth

Man was originally created in the image and after the likeness of God, and he fell through sin, and, as a consequence of his sin, lost his spiritual life, becoming dead in trespasses and sins, and became subject to the power of the devil.

What This Means

  • • Image of God: We were created to reflect God's character, creativity, and authority
  • • Spiritual Death: Sin severed our connection with God, leaving us spiritually dead
  • • Under Satan's Power: Apart from God's intervention, we remain in bondage

Key Scripture References

Genesis 1:26-27

God created man in His own image—the foundation of human dignity and purpose

Genesis 2:17

Man was given dominion and warned that disobedience would bring death

Genesis 3:1-6

The serpent deceived Eve, and Adam willfully disobeyed—humanity fell

1 John 3:8

Sin is of the devil—apart from God's intervention, we remain in his power

E

The Biblical Role of Women and Men

Equal in Worth, Distinct in Design

Creation

Both man and woman were created by God, equal as persons, yet distinct by divine design in their manhood and womanhood.

Genesis 1:26-27 – Equal in God's image

Genesis 2:18-23 – Woman as helper

1 Cor. 11:3, 8-12 – Divine order

1 Tim. 2:11-13 – Creation order

Marriage & Family

God established heterosexual marriage as the only acceptable pattern and ordained men to lead the family in love.

Genesis 2:18-25 – Marriage instituted

Mark 10:6-9 – What God joined

Ephesians 5:22-33 – Christ & Church

1 Peter 3:1-7 – Mutual honor

Redemption & Ministry

Women and men alike through personal faith in Christ enjoy the benefits of Christ's redeeming work and are called to minister.

Galatians 3:26-29 – Equal in salvation

1 Peter 4:10 – Each one serve

1 Cor. 12:4-31 – Diversity of gifts

2 Tim. 2:15-16 – Study to show approved

Women in Ministry

God desires to use women in significant ministry: in personal evangelism (John 4:39), teaching women and children (Rom. 12:7), service ministries (James 1:27; Rom. 16:1-2), assisting husbands (Rom. 16:3), family ministry (2 Tim. 1:5, 3:15; Prov. 31:10-31), and counseling other women (Titus 2:4-5).

F

The First Advent

Christ Came to Save

Core Truth

The eternal Son of God came into this world to: manifest God to men, fulfill prophecy, and become the Redeemer of a lost world. To this end He was born of the virgin, received a human body and sinless human nature.

His Incarnation

Luke 1:30-35

Born of the virgin Mary, conceived by the Holy Spirit

John 1:1-2, 14, 18

The Word was God... the Word became flesh

Philippians 2:5-8

He humbled Himself, taking the form of a servant

Isaiah 9:6

Wonderful, Counselor, Mighty God, Everlasting Father

Hebrews 4:15

Tempted in all points, yet without sin

His Mission

John 1:11

He came to His own, but His own did not receive Him

John 3:16

God so loved the world that He gave His only Son

1 Timothy 2:6

He gave Himself as a ransom for all

His Substitutionary Death

John 1:29

Behold, the Lamb of God who takes away the sin of the world

Romans 3:25-26

God presented Him as a propitiation through faith in His blood

2 Corinthians 5:14

One died for all, therefore all died

Hebrews 10:5-14

By one offering He perfected forever those being sanctified

1 Peter 3:18

The just for the unjust, to bring us to God

His Resurrection & Present Ministry

John 20:20

He showed them His hands and His side—risen bodily

Philippians 3:20-21

He will transform our bodies like His glorious body

Hebrews 1:3

He purged our sins and sat down at the right hand

Hebrews 7:25

He ever lives to make intercession for us

Ephesians 1:22-23

Head over all things to the church, His body

1 John 2:1

We have an Advocate with the Father—Jesus Christ

Discussion Questions

Question 1

What does it mean to be created in God's image, and how does sin affect that image in us?

Question 2

How do complementary roles between men and women reflect God's wisdom rather than inequality?

Question 3

Why was it necessary for Christ to be both fully God and fully man to accomplish our salvation?

Question 4

How does Christ's present ministry as our Intercessor and Advocate affect your daily life?
