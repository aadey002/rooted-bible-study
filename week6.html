Week 6: End Times

Second Coming, Rapture, Tribulation, Millennium

Overview

This week examines what the Bible teaches about future events and the return of Jesus Christ.

1. The Second Coming

Jesus Christ will return personally and visibly to earth to establish His kingdom and judge the world.

"This Jesus, who was taken up from you into heaven, will come in the same way as you saw him go into heaven." - Acts 1:11

2. The Rapture

Christ will return to gather His church, both the dead in Christ and living believers, to be with Him forever.

"For the Lord himself will descend from heaven with a cry of command, with the voice of an archangel, and with the sound of the trumpet of God." - 1 Thessalonians 4:16

3. The Tribulation

A period of unprecedented trouble will come upon the earth as God pours out His judgment on a rebellious world.

"For then there will be great tribulation, such as has not been from the beginning of the world until now, no, and never will be." - Matthew 24:21

4. The Millennium

Christ will reign on earth for a thousand years, fulfilling Gods promises to Israel and establishing peace on earth.

"They came to life and reigned with Christ for a thousand years." - Revelation 20:4
