Week 5: Spiritual Gifts

Spiritual Gifts, Great Commission, Evangelism, Discipleship

Overview

This week explores the gifts God gives to believers and our mission to make disciples of all nations.

1. Spiritual Gifts

The Holy Spirit distributes spiritual gifts to every believer for the building up of the body of Christ.

"Now there are varieties of gifts, but the same Spirit; and there are varieties of service, but the same Lord." - 1 Corinthians 12:4-5

2. The Great Commission

Jesus commanded His followers to make disciples of all nations, baptizing and teaching them to obey His commands.

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit." - Matthew 28:19

3. Evangelism

Evangelism is the proclamation of the gospel to those who have not heard or believed in Jesus Christ.

"How beautiful are the feet of those who preach the good news!" - Romans 10:15

4. Discipleship

Discipleship is the process of helping believers grow in their faith and become mature followers of Christ.

"And what you have heard from me in the presence of many witnesses entrust to faithful men, who will be able to teach others also." - 2 Timothy 2:2
