King of Kings Returns to Reign

Core Truth

After the tribulation, Christ will return visibly and bodily to earth with His saints. He will defeat His enemies at Armageddon, bind Satan for 1,000 years, and establish His millennial kingdom, reigning from David's throne in Jerusalem.

His Return

In that day His feet will stand on the Mount of Olives

They will see the Son of Man coming on the clouds of heaven with power and great glory

Revelation 19:11-16

The Millennium

Revelation 20:1-6

Satan bound 1,000 years; they lived and reigned with Christ for a thousand years

Isaiah 11:6-9

The wolf shall dwell with the lamb... the earth shall be full of the knowledge of the LORD

Luke 1:32-33

He will reign over the house of Jacob forever, and of His kingdom there will be no end

Core Truth

We are stewards‚Äînot owners‚Äîof all that God has entrusted to us: time, talents, and treasure. The first portion belongs to God. We honor Him with proportionate, willing, and cheerful giving, recognizing that everything we have comes from His hand.
